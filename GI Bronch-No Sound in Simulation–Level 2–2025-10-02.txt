GI Bronch – No Sound in Simulation
Access Level: 2
Date: 2025-10-12
The simulation runs normally, but there is no sound during operation.
Troubleshooting Steps:
- Check volume in simulation screen – locate the volume control icon and ensure sound is not muted.
- If still no sound, exit to Windows.
- In Windows, verify the speaker icon is not muted, volume slider is up, and correct playback device is selected.
