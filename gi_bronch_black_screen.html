GI Bronch - Black Screen - L1 Procedure

TOKEN-GB-BLACK-123

  1. Check keyboard LED is on.
  2. Listen for internal fans; no sound may mean PC is off.
  3. Verify main power switch on the back is on and illuminated.
  4. Press the red front power button above the scope connector.
  5. Reseat monitor power, video, and USB cables.
  6. Toggle monitor power or reseat the power cable.
